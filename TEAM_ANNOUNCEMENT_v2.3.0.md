## 🎯 What is Master SOP?

Master SOP is a **complete guide to software development best practices** covering:

✅ **Development Standards** - SPARC methodology, agent/skill development, architecture
✅ **Quality Standards** - Testing requirements, test coverage targets, verification
✅ **Documentation Standards** - What to document, how to document, when to document
✅ **Deployment Standards** - Safe deployments, rollback procedures, monitoring
✅ **Collaboration Standards** - Code review, communication, team workflows
✅ **Security Standards** - Secrets management, security gates, compliance

**Plus**: Ready-to-use templates, checklists, and audit tools

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Is this mandatory?
**A**: Core standards yes. Customizations by project. This is designed to improve quality - it's in everyone's interest.

### Q: Can we customize for our project?
**A**: Absolutely! Use PROJECT_MASTER_SOP_TEMPLATE.md to create your project's customizations.

### Q: How long does adoption take?
**A**: 1-2 weeks for project setup. Full adoption with metrics tracking: 3-6 months.

### Q: What if we disagree with a standard?
**A**: Great! Propose alternatives and document your reasoning. Standards should improve quality, so let's discuss.

### Q: How do we verify compliance?
**A**: Use MASTER_SOP_VERIFICATION_CHECKLIST.md. Quick check (5 min) to deep audit (2-3 hours).

### Q: Who can I ask questions?
**A**: Your team lead, tech lead, or the SOP Committee (contact your engineering manager).

## 💡 Success Tips

### Start Small
Begin with one project and learn from it. Don't try to implement everywhere at once.

### Get Team Input
Customize for your context. Your team knows what works best for your situation.

### Track Early
Use verification checklist early to establish baseline. Monthly reviews show progress.

### Share Learnings
What works for your team might work for others. Share successful patterns and lessons learned.

### Ask Questions
Standards aren't rules carved in stone. If something doesn't work, let's discuss and improve it.
