## FEATURE: Risk Management & Position Sizing

### Overview
Enterprise-grade risk management tools ensuring traders never exceed their risk tolerance.

### Risk Controls

**Position Limits**
- Maximum position size
- Maximum exposure per sector
- Maximum correlation
- Concentration limits

**Stop-Loss Automation**
- Manual stop-loss orders
- Trailing stops
- Percentage-based stops
- Volatility-based stops

**Account Protection**
- Daily loss limits
- Weekly loss limits
- Monthly loss limits
- Auto-halt trading on limits

**Margin Management**
- Real-time margin usage
- Margin call alerts
- Automatic position reduction
- Leverage limits

### Position Sizing Models

**Fixed Dollar Model**
- Risk $X per trade
- Calculates position size based on stop distance

**Fixed Percentage Model**
- Risk Y% of account per trade
- Scales with account growth/decline

**Kelly Criterion**
- Mathematical position sizing
- Based on win rate and profit factor

**Volatility-Based**
- Size based on ATR or standard deviation
- Adapts to market conditions
