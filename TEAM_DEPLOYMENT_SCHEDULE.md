## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This deployment schedule coordinates the phased rollout of the Aurigraph Agent Architecture across all Aurigraph teams, prioritizing critical projects and ensuring proper support coverage throughout the deployment.

**Deployment Strategy**:
- **Phase 1** (Week 1): Priority projects (HMS, DLT Services)
- **Phase 2** (Week 2): Important projects (ESG, Website)
- **Phase 3** (Week 3): Standard projects (Internal Tools, Data)
- **Phase 4** (Week 4+): All remaining teams and optimization

**Priority Sequence**: HMS → DLT Services → ESG → Website → Internal Tools → Data & Analytics → Mobile → Others

## ROLLBACK PROCEDURES

### Project-Specific Rollback

If a specific project encounters critical issues:

**Immediate Actions** (0-2 hours):
1. Champion notifies core team
2. Stop new installations for that project
3. Document issue
4. Communicate to affected team

**Removal** (2-24 hours):
1. Provide removal instructions to team
2. Verify removal
3. Restore previous workflow

**Re-Deployment** (1-2 weeks):
1. Fix issue
2. Test with champion
3. Re-deploy when ready

### Organization-Wide Rollback

If critical issues affect multiple projects:

**Trigger**: >50% of users affected or security vulnerability
**Action**: See DEPLOYMENT_EXECUTION_PLAN.md "Rollback Plan" section

## CONTINGENCY PLANNING

### Behind Schedule Scenarios

**Scenario 1: Project Adoption <80% by End of Week**
- **Action**: Champion + core team investigation
- **Resolution**: Targeted outreach, extra workshop, management escalation
- **Timeline**: 3-5 days to resolution

**Scenario 2: Training Session No-Shows**
- **Action**: Record session, share video
- **Resolution**: Office hours, one-on-one support, async materials
- **Timeline**: Ongoing

**Scenario 3: Champion Unavailable**
- **Action**: Assign backup champion
- **Resolution**: Core team direct support, new champion training
- **Timeline**: <24 hours
